COMPANY OVERVIEW

Amna Enterprises was established in 2005 as a sole proprietorship company, initially trading in textile items. Since then company have observed great portfolio change and vast product range, as in late 2020’s Amna Enterprises is an established name in import, export, general order supplies and related services.

With established suppliers in China, Middle East, Asia and many parts of Europe, we can supply you with best quality, competitive price and exceptional delivery time in products such as Fire-Safety-Security, Natural Stone, and related machinery.

Further, Amna Enterprises fair turn over remains with exporting of finished textile products to various multinationals in Europe, USA and Canada. We offer all the related services for all our main product range, from designing, supplying, installation/commissioning and after sales services.

Amna Enterprises believes and lives a culture where everybody wins. We regard and provide ample opportunity to our employees, best services to our customers and full commitment to our suppliers. Being compliant is the basis of our business and being sustainable is our motive which derives our business.

Amna Enterprises continues to grow ever day thanks to the confidence our clients have in us. We cover many industries such as textile , pharma, chemical , construction, energy, business services, consumer products and transportation.
