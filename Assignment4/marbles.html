MARBLE AND GRANITE

We deal in all kinds of marble and onyx products that include monolama blocks, marble slabs, kitchen tops, vanity tops, steps, raisers, marble tiles and cut to size works. We also manufacture handicrafts in wide range and quality that in circles marble sinks, pedestals, marble medallions, borders, splash backs, inlays, pillars, fire places, fountains, flower vases, flower pots, urns, bowls and lamps.
We have our own quarry of yellow sand stone/Imperial Gold , yellow lime stone /Sindh Marpha, Black and gold , honey onyx and medium green onyx. We are always searching for new stones and are working on different red and beige color stones.
We also import exotic stones from Afghanistan like pearl white, galaxy white marble and re export to different parts of the world after value addition.
We have special expertise in Pakistani Onyx in dark green onyx, light green Onyx , and white Onyx. We can supply blocks for making big slabs, cut to size tiles and boulders for handi crafts, gift craft and monuments making.
